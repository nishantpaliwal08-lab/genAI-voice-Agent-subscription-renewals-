# Problem Statement & Business Context – GPS Subscription Renewals

Logistics businesses that depend on GPS tracking products often operate on recurring subscription plans.

A large part of retention depends not on product usage alone, but on repeated outbound follow-up with vehicle owners and fleet operators.

In many cases, renewals do not happen automatically because:

* payment is delayed
* operator attention is elsewhere
* product value is remembered only when explicitly discussed
* previous service issues influence willingness to renew

This creates a recurring outbound calling workflow where human agents repeatedly explain renewal status, pricing, validity, and available plan options.

The GPS renewal use case is therefore a strong candidate for conversational AI prototyping because:

* the objective is narrow
* the product is already familiar to the customer
* objections are repetitive
* conversational patterns are observable across calls

The goal of this prototype is not production deployment.

The goal is to show how a PM can move from:

call recordings → conversational flow → lightweight AI prototype

within a few hours.
