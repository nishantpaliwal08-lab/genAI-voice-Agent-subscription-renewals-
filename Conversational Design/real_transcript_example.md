# Real Transcript Example

Below is a shortened renewal conversation excerpt used before prototype design.

---

**Agent:** Namaste sir, Wheelseye se baat kar raha hoon.
**Customer:** Haan bolo.

**Agent:** Sir aapki gaadi ka GPS renewal pending hai.
**Customer:** Kitne ka hai?

**Agent:** Same one-year plan available hai.
**Customer:** Mehenga lag raha hai.

**Agent:** Agar yearly abhi convenient nahi ho toh short validity bhi available hai.
**Customer:** Theek hai baad mein batata hoon.

---

## Why this transcript matters

Even this short exchange contains:

* acceptance
* renewal awareness
* price curiosity
* objection
* fallback possibility

That is enough to design first-stage conversational logic.
